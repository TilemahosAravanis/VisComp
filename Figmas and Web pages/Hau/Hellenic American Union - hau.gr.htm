Founded in 1957, the Hellenic American Union is a public-service, educational and cultural institution with an international focus and strong community commitment. We partner with the best to provide people with exceptional, proven opportunities for personal and professional development, whatever their background.
Learn more

Training

The Hellenic American Union provides a rich range of seminars that cover key professional competencies and life skills that will help you realize your creative and professional potential, advance your career, and enhance the quality of your life.
Learn more

Learning

The Hellenic American Union has been a leader in designing and delivering life-long learning programs for generations of Greek and international audiences. Discover our language-learning and exam-preparation programs for English and Greek.
Learn more

Exams

Since 1957, the Hellenic American Union has administered open, fair, and secure language examinations to more than two million candidates throughout Greece and the broader region.
Learn more

Awards & Distinctions:

The Hellenic American Union is one of over 250 worldwide institutionsand the only one in Greecewhose foreign language and teacher training programs are accredited by the CEA. In earning accreditation for its programs, the Hellenic American Union joins the ranks of institutions such as Boston University, Georgetown University, and the University of Pennsylvania whose English-language programs have been similarly accredited.
